A Short intro

This project was inspired by the German Enigma machine which was a device used for encryption during World War II. It used rotors to scramble messages which could only be decrypted with a matching Enigma machine. The machine was used extensively by the german military to send secret messages, but was eventually cracked by codebreakers at Bletchley Park in the UK.

So, unless there are codebreakers trying to bust your little message encrypted here, you're safe!
